### Section 9 — Acknowledgments

Each row: one checkbox (`ackN`) + one initials text field (`initialsN`).  
**Checkbox rule:** check if the corresponding `ack_*_at` column is non-NULL.  
**Initials rule:** derive from `buyer_name` — first letter of each word, uppercase (e.g. "Carlos Lebron Rivera" → `"CLR"`).  
**Fallback:** if the dedicated ack column does not exist yet (ack4, ack5 below), check if `buyer_signed_at` is non-NULL.

| PDF field pair | PDF label (abbreviated) | DB ack column |
|---|---|---|
| `ack1` / `initials1` | "I have read and understand this entire Agreement" | `ack_full_agreement_at` |
| `ack2` / `initials2` | "I understand that this $300 deposit is NON-REFUNDABLE" | `ack_payment_authorization_at` |
| `ack3` / `initials3` | "I understand the pickup policy and will coordinate my pickup date" | `ack_pickup_acceptance_at` |
| `ack4` / `initials4` | "I acknowledge applicable sales tax will be collected at final payment" | fallback `buyer_signed_at` (no dedicated column) |
| `ack5` / `initials5` | "I understand my reservation is NOT confirmed until deposit verified + seller countersigns" | fallback `buyer_signed_at` (no dedicated column) |
| `ack6` / `initials6` | "I confirm I am at least 18 years of age and all information is accurate" | `ack_age_attestation_at` |
| `ack7` / `initials7` | "I understand my electronic/typed signature has the same legal effect as handwritten" | `ack_esign_valid_at` |

> Note: `ack_identity_attestation_at` and `ack_pre_dispute_contact_at` are Wave E/H web-form acks that do not have corresponding PDF checkboxes in this deposit agreement template. They are timestamped in the DB only.
