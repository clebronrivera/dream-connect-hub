Brand

Dream Puppies — the doing-business-as for Dream Enterprises LLC. Family-operated across Florida and North Carolina. The voice is warm, confident, slightly mischievous; never corporate, never cute-for-cute's-sake.

Voice

Warm. Plain-spoken.

Family talking to a future family. We write like we'd text — short sentences, real specifics, zero jargon.

Tone shifts

Confident, not pushy.

Hero copy is bold. Forms are reassuring. FAQs are honest. We don't oversell — the puppies do.

Things we don't say

No "fur babies."

No "doodles 'til I die." No "puppy parents." No fake urgency, no fake scarcity, no flat-rate guarantees we can't honor.

Buttons

Pill-shaped, all-caps, slight letter-spacing. The primary has a chunky drop-shadow in --primary-deep — it reads as a sticker, not a UI button. Two surfaces (light/dark) and four intents.

Tags & status

All tags are solid-fill pills with a leading dot for status. They never carry hover states — they're labels, not actions.

Reserve-a-spot flow

Three steps. The price never appears until the final screen, where the deposit is calculated based on whether the litter is born yet.

STEP 1

Pick litter + spot

User clicks an open slot in a litter card. Modal opens preloaded with litter, breed, due date, expected coat range.

STEP 2

Tell us about your home

Name, contact, household notes, preferences (sex, color leaning), pickup or transport. Reassuring micro-copy: this isn't a contract yet.

STEP 3 · DEPOSIT

Confirm + deposit

Total puppy price shown. Deposit auto-calculated:
