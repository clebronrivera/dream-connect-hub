❤️ Don't Panic Guide
Vomiting
Usually Normal:
White foam on empty stomach. Yellow bile in the morning (try a bedtime snack). Undigested food from eating too fast. Single episode, no other symptoms.
Call Your Vet:
More than 3 times in 24 hours. Blood (red streaks or dark coffee-ground look). Combined with lethargy or diarrhea. Won't eat or drink. Under 12 weeks old.
Coat & Grooming
Some shedding during adjustment is normal. Brush daily to prevent matting (Goldendoodles have curly coats!). First professional grooming at 12–16 weeks. Excessive scratching may mean allergies or fleas — check with your vet.
Energy & Behavior
Puppies sleep 18–20 hours a day — completely normal! Initial shyness in a new home is expected. Give Buddy 2–3 quiet days to adjust. If he won't eat or play at all after 48 hours, contact your vet.
